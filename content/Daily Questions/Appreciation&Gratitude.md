# Appreciation & Gratitude

### Questions

1. What's something small I did recently that made you smile? <!-- Positive Reinforcement -->
2. What is your favorite memory of us from the past month? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
3. What's one thing you admire about me that you don't say enough? <!-- Strengths-Based Psychology -->
4. How do you prefer to receive appreciation? <!-- Love Languages Theory -->
5. What's a little thing I do that makes your day better? <!-- Observational Psychology -->
6. When have you felt most appreciated by me? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
7. What's a way we could celebrate small wins together? <!-- Behavioral Psychology -->
8. What's something ordinary about our life together that you love? <!-- Mindfulness Psychology -->
9. How does gratitude affect your mood? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
10. What's one small thing you're grateful for today? <!-- Gratitude Practice -->

11. What's a favorite moment we've shared that you often think about? <!-- Memory Recall Psychology -->
12. What's one thing you love about our relationship? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
13. How can I better show my appreciation for you? <!-- Communication Theory -->
14. What's a tradition we have (or could create) to celebrate us? <!-- Rituals & Traditions Psychology -->
15. How does it feel when your efforts are recognized? <!-- Self-Worth Psychology -->
16. What act of kindness from me stuck with you recently? <!-- Altruism Research -->
17. What are three little things you're grateful for about today? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
18. What do you think is one of my unsung strengths? <!-- Strengths-Based Psychology -->
19. How would you describe our "good times" together? <!-- Nostalgia Psychology -->
20. What "small win" would you like us to celebrate this week? <!-- Motivation Psychology -->

21. How does expressing gratitude change your perception of challenges? <!-- Cognitive Reframing -->
22. What's something I did this week that made you feel seen? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
23. How do you think appreciation strengthens love? <!-- Relationship Psychology -->
24. What's one thing I did at the start of our relationship that made you fall for me? <!-- Nostalgia Psychology -->
25. How do you think our relationship has evolved because of mutual appreciation? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
26. What's your favorite way to say "thank you" to someone you love? <!-- Love Languages Theory -->
27. What's a quirky or silly thing about me you're secretly grateful for? <!-- Play Theory -->
28. What was a moment when you felt truly cherished? <!-- Secure Attachment Theory -->
29. How has your view of gratitude changed over your life? <!-- Developmental Psychology -->
30. What's something about us you never want to take for granted? <!-- Existential Psychology -->

31. How does gratitude show up in your love for me? <!-- Emotion-Focused Therapy -->
32. What's something about your own personality you're grateful for? <!-- Positive Self-Reflection -->
33. How could we build more moments of appreciation into our routine? <!-- Habit Formation Psychology -->
34. What physical gesture from me feels most loving to you? <!-- Somatic Psychology -->
35. What's a "little thing" you miss when we're apart? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
36. When you imagine our future, what about our connection makes you feel grateful? <!-- Positive Visualization -->
37. What's one thing you think we do better than most couples? <!-- Strengths-Based Psychology -->
38. What's a hardship we faced that you're now grateful for? <!-- Post-Traumatic Growth -->
39. How does focusing on gratitude affect how you view our relationship? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
40. What would life feel like without appreciation? <!-- Existential Psychology -->

41. How do you think expressing gratitude builds trust? <!-- Trust Psychology -->
42. What's one way you'd love for me to surprise you with appreciation? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
43. What's an example of a small thing I could do that would mean a lot to you? <!-- Behavioral Psychology -->
44. How would you feel if you wrote me a gratitude letter? <!-- Expressive Writing Therapy -->
45. How can we create a "gratitude ritual" just for us? <!-- Rituals & Traditions Psychology -->
46. What's something about our relationship you don't say thank you for enough? <!-- Self-Awareness Psychology -->
47. How has our relationship helped you become more grateful in general? <!-- Developmental Psychology -->
48. What's something you hope to always appreciate about me, no matter how long we're together? <!-- Long-Term Relationship Psychology -->
49. What's one way we could make gratitude a more natural part of our conversations? <!-- Habit Formation Psychology -->
50. What's the biggest gift our relationship has given you that you're thankful for? <!-- Gratitude Practice -->
