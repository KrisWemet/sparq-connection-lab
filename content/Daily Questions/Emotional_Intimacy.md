# Emotional Intimacy Questions

## Easy

1. What's one little thing today that made you smile? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
2. When do you feel most relaxed around me? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
3. What's a small compliment you've never forgotten? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
4. If you could bottle up a feeling with me, what would it be? <!-- Emotion-Focused Therapy -->
5. What does "emotional safety" mean to you? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
6. How do you prefer to be comforted when you're stressed? <!-- Love Languages -->
7. What's a song that always lifts your mood? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
8. When do you feel most like yourself with me? <!-- Self-Concept Theory -->
9. How would you describe a perfect lazy Sunday together? <!-- Emotion-Focused Therapy -->
10. If you had to pick a color to describe our emotional connection, what color would it be? <!-- Color Psychology -->

## Medium

11. What's something you loved about your childhood that you want to bring into our relationship? <!-- Inner Child Work -->
12. How do you know when you're starting to feel overwhelmed? <!-- Somatic Therapy -->
13. What is a non-verbal way you like to show love? <!-- Nonverbal Communication Psychology -->
14. What's one thing you wish you felt safer sharing with others? <!-- Vulnerability Research -->
15. When you're upset, what do you wish people would do (or not do)? <!-- Emotion Regulation Theory -->
16. If our relationship were a weather pattern, what would it be today? <!-- Metaphor Therapy -->
17. What's a small way I can emotionally support you this week? <!-- Supportive Communication Theory -->
18. What's one fear about intimacy you've overcome (or are working on)? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
19. When did you first realize emotional connection mattered to you? <!-- Developmental Psychology -->
20. What's a "secret hope" you have for our emotional connection? <!-- Hope Theory -->

## Deeper

21. What's an emotional scar you carry that you wish I understood better? <!-- Trauma-Informed Care -->
22. What's your biggest fear about being vulnerable? <!-- Vulnerability Research -->
23. How do you handle emotional pain? <!-- Coping Mechanisms Research -->
24. What's one way past relationships have shaped how you trust today? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
25. What's something about my emotional world you want to understand more? <!-- Emotionally Focused Therapy -->
26. When have you felt the most emotionally seen by me? <!-- Mirror Neuron Theory -->
27. What's a core emotional need you have in a relationship? <!-- Needs Theory -->
28. If emotional intimacy were a muscle, what "workout" would help strengthen ours? <!-- Metaphor Therapy -->
29. How do you experience anger — internally and externally? <!-- Emotion Regulation Theory -->
30. What triggers feelings of shame for you in relationships? <!-- Shame Resilience Theory -->

## Hard

31. What's one thing you fear would make me love you less if I knew? <!-- Vulnerability Research -->
32. What unspoken rules did you learn about emotions growing up? <!-- Family Systems Theory -->
33. How do you experience loneliness, even when you're not alone? <!-- Existential Psychology -->
34. What part of yourself do you keep most guarded, even with me? <!-- Parts Work (IFS) -->
35. In what ways have I unintentionally hurt your feelings before? <!-- Nonviolent Communication -->
36. What emotional patterns do you notice repeating in our relationship? <!-- Relationship Systems Intelligence -->
37. How do you self-protect when you're scared of being hurt? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
38. What's a painful memory you've never shared with me but feel ready to? <!-- Trauma-Informed Care -->
39. If your heart had a "closed" sign and an "open" sign, what flips the switch? <!-- Emotion-Focused Therapy -->
40. What's something you believe about yourself that you're working to heal? <!-- Cognitive Behavioral Therapy -->

## Very Deep

41. If our emotional bond was a living being, what would it need right now? <!-- Metaphor Therapy -->
42. How do you want me to love you when you're hurting? <!-- Compassionate Inquiry -->
43. What's a tender part of you that you rarely show the world? <!-- Inner Child Work -->
44. What is a story you tell yourself about love that no longer serves you? <!-- Narrative Therapy -->
45. What makes you feel emotionally "at home" with someone? <!-- Attachment Theory -->
46. How do you feel about forgiveness — giving it and receiving it? <!-- Forgiveness Psychology -->
47. What emotional risks are you willing to take to deepen our connection? <!-- Risk-Taking Research -->
48. How do you hope we grow emotionally over the next year? <!-- Growth Mindset -->
49. If we faced a major challenge, what emotional strengths would we rely on? <!-- Resilience Psychology -->
50. What's one thing you hope I *never* forget about your emotional world? <!-- Emotionally Focused Therapy -->
