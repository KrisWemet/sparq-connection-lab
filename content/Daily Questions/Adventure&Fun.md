5. What's your go-to karaoke song? Would you sing it with me? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
6. If we could have any animal as a pet together, what would you choose? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
7. What's a childhood activity you miss that we could recreate together? <!-- Narrative Therapy -->
8. What's a fun tradition you'd like to start with us? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
9. If we could learn a new skill together, what would you want it to be? <!-- Positive Psychology -->
10. What's the most adventurous food you've tried? What would you like me to try? <!-- Positive Psychology -->

## Mini Challenges

- Try a completely new activity together this weekend
- Plan a surprise mini-adventure for your partner
- Take turns teaching each other something you're good at
- Create a couple's bucket list with 10 adventures you want to experience together
- Recreate your favorite childhood game and play it together
- Try a new cuisine neither of you has experienced before
- Spend 30 minutes together exploring one of your partner's interests!
