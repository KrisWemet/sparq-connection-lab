Key Difference

A (Stacked Cards) — Organized, scannable, modular. Each growth dimension has its own card. Feels like a well-organized dashboard. Growth is visible through data-informed cards.

B (Peter-Led Narrative) — Peter IS the dashboard. He greets you, contextualizes today in terms of yesterday, and your growth thread reads like a journal you co-wrote. Feels less like a dashboard, more like opening a conversation with someone who knows you.
