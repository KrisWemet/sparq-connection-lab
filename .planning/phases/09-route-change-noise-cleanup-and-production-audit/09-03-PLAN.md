# Plan 09-03 — Focused Production Audit And Ranked Findings

Wave: 3

Depends on:
- `09-02`

## Objective

Perform a focused audit and document at least five real bugs, issues, or redundancies, ranked for follow-up.

## Scope

- prioritize the primary signup-driven production path first
- then audit operator-facing production risks
- then include obvious redundancies or dead or legacy paths
- capture only real, evidence-backed findings

## Required Output For Each Finding

- title
- severity: `blocker`, `high`, `medium`, or `low`
- affected files
- why it matters
- recommended fix
- whether it should be fixed `now`, `later`, or `ignored`

## Expected Changes

- a durable audit artifact with at least five ranked findings
- no obligation to fix those findings in this phase unless they are tiny and directly tied to the route-noise cleanup

## Explicitly Out Of Scope

- speculative issues
- broad redesign
- random polishing
- sweeping fixes for all findings

## Verification

- direct code inspection
- production and operator evidence review where relevant
- ranked findings artifact checked into planning docs

## Success Criteria

- at least five credible findings are documented and ranked
- the findings are grounded in the supported production path, operator risks, or obvious dead or legacy redundancy
- the audit gives a clear next queue for follow-up work
