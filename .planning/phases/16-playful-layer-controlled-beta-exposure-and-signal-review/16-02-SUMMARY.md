# 16-02 Summary

Phase 16 Wave 2 reviewed the core funnel and playful stream together using production `analytics_events` plus targeted live cohort feedback.

The signal readout is positive but small:
- playful events were still visible for the pre-cutoff cohort
- post-cutoff users emitted no playful events
- the primary funnel still completed through Day 1 after the gate
- qualitative feedback on both playful surfaces described the layer as warm, optional, and non-intrusive

There is no evidence that the playful layer is hurting the primary path. There is also not enough sample size yet to claim a measurable lift in completion or retention.
