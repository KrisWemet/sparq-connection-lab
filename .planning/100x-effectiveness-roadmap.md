# Sparq 100x Effectiveness Roadmap

**Goal:** Make each session deeper (A) AND make the practice escape the app into real life (C).

**Core diagnosis:** Current Sparq touches cognitive understanding and weak behavioral practice. Missing: emotional activation / felt sense, and real-world context / in-the-moment application.

---

## The Five Ideas

### 1. Rehearsal Room ← START HERE
**Mechanism:** Behavioral rehearsal (CBT/exposure therapy)
**Problem it solves:** Users understand what to do but their nervous system takes over in the real moment. The insight evaporates.
**The idea:** Before a difficult conversation, user tells Peter what they need to say. Peter *becomes the partner* — responds with realistic resistance, distraction, or defensiveness. User practices the actual words, multiple times, until it doesn't feel scary. By the time it happens in real life, it's not the first time their nervous system has done it.
**Why first:** Highest-leverage mechanism change. Unique in the market. Gives Peter his most powerful role — not just companion but sparring partner for real life.
**Status:** Not started

---

### 2. Real-Time Capture ("I just need 90 seconds")
**Mechanism:** In-the-moment feedback loop
**Problem it solves:** Insight happens at 7am. Triggering moment happens at 6pm. No bridge. Learning never meets the moment.
**The idea:** One-tap entry from lockscreen/widget. Peter asks 3 micro-questions immediately after a significant moment: *What just happened? What did you feel in your body? What do you wish you'd done differently?* 90 seconds. Processed that night. Becomes the next morning's session content.
**Why second:** Makes the daily loop follow the user's actual life instead of running on a fixed schedule disconnected from what's happening.
**Status:** Not started

---

### 3. Partner Telepathy
**Mechanism:** Simultaneous perspective reveal
**Problem it solves:** Insights stay individual. Nothing forces them to land *between* the two people as a shared experience.
**The idea:** 1-2x per week, both partners independently answer the same question about each other — *"What do you think your partner needs most from you right now?"* Both answers revealed simultaneously. The gap between what they think and what's true becomes the curriculum.
**Why third:** The act of doing this IS the connection practice. When right, deeply bonding. When wrong, more revealing than anything Peter could teach.
**Status:** Not started

---

### 4. Designed Real-World Rituals
**Mechanism:** Physical world artifact design
**Problem it solves:** App output is insights and micro-actions. Neither has gravity. Neither is a *thing* in the world.
**The idea:** At journey completion, Peter designs a specific, personalized ritual for this couple — based on their patterns, attachment styles, and what emerged. Not generic. Specific. The ritual lives in the world. The app designs it and gets out of the way.
**Why fourth:** Creates real-life architecture that makes the app unnecessary for that piece of growth. Counterintuitively creates the deepest loyalty.
**Status:** Not started

---

### 5. The Compound Reveal
**Mechanism:** Evidence of change in user's own words
**Problem it solves:** Change is invisible from the inside. Users quit because they can't see they're changing.
**The idea:** At journey start, Peter captures a hyper-specific "before" snapshot — actual language, specific fears, patterns described. Locked away. At Day 14 and completion, Peter surfaces it back. *"30 days ago you told me you always shut down when criticized. Here's what you said three days ago."* Not a data dashboard — a story.
**Why fifth:** Makes users stay for years, not weeks. Requires data accumulation from the earlier features to be most powerful.
**Status:** Not started

---

## Build Order

1. Rehearsal Room
2. Real-Time Capture
3. Partner Telepathy
4. Designed Real-World Rituals
5. Compound Reveal

---

## Design Principles for All Five

- Peter is the delivery mechanism for all of them — his voice, warmth, and intelligence is what makes these feel safe rather than clinical
- None of these should feel like features. They should feel like Peter doing something new.
- Every one of these must work solo-first. Partner features are enhancements, not requirements.
- The 5-minute promise is sacred. Rehearsal Room and Telepathy may go longer by user choice — never by default.
